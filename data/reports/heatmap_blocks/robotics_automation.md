Dominant catalyst type: event+structural. The boost from event catalysts to `struct_nato_rearmament` creates the asymmetry — defense robotics demand is less rate-sensitive and less EV-cycle-sensitive than the mainstream industrial automation thesis.

**Non-obvious finding — Momentum (69.3) is the weakest top-8 score; the catalyst is running ahead of price.**
